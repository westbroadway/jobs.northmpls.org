  • The Chemical Process/Boiler House Manager is
primarily responsible for the operation of the
primary raw material processing segment of the
manufacturing operation along with the plant steam
boiler operation. The manager would focus on process
improvement and the reduction of process variation.
Working through the boiler house department
supervisor and boiler and stills team, the Chemical
Process/Boiler House Manager would develop and
maintain comprehensive procedures, policies and
systems to improve safety, training, quality and
efficiency.
  • Develops and manages oxidation operations equipment
integrity programs, (tanks piping, hot oil systems,
etc.).
  • Coordinates asphalt qualification trial plans and
provides plant support for the qualification process
including trials.
  • Identifies and provides guidance for implementing
oxidation operations best practices.
  • Provides leadership for identified oxidation
operations process improvement activities.
  • Assures raw material specifications meet the
business needs.
  • Generates oxidation operations quality reports,
monitors performance and provides follow-up
appropriate to assure quality standards are met.
  • Drives measurement variation reduction associated
with asphalt test procedures.
The Chemical Process/Boiler House Manager is also
responsible for maintaining excellence in developing,
executing and controlling assigned projects to achieve
site goals. Through effective application of engineering
expertise, the individual will conduct analysis and
investigations pertaining to construction of new
equipment and processes, and alteration or modification
of existing facilities to optimize productive capability
of the plant. Project recommendations are made taking
into consideration such factors as cost, structure,
maintainability, facility of production and availability
of materials and equipment with emphasis on safety and
optimum performance.

Specific duties include, but are not limited to:
  • Management of the oxidation operation and boilers as
outlined above.
  • Focus on the design and/or improvement of electrical
and mechanical systems that manage, protect and
improve our plant equipment and processes.
  • Manage electrical and mechanical and process
projects from conception to completion, utilizing
both in-house maintenance personnel and outside
contractors.
  • Reports to the Plant Manager.
