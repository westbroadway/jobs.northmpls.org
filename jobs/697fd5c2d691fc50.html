Job Description

We have a wonderful opportunity for an evening nurse looking for full or part-time hours in the northern Minneapolis area. Our client is an adorable and interactive, 16 month old boy whose smile lights up the room. He is trach and ventilator dependent and is in need of an experienced pediatric nurse. Shifts available include Monday, Wednesday, Friday and Saturday evenings from 3 pm to 11 pm.

We are looking for RNs and LPNs with at least 1 year of nursing experience to provide excellent care to this client in need. We offer extensive pediatric, trach, and vent training and orientation to this case.

Come work for an organization where you will be rewarded for your hard work and excellence, and be treated with respect and appreciation. Active nursing license and one year experience are required.
