Job Description

We are looking for medical production operators to work in a successful medical component company in the Twin Cities. Position will be working in clean room assembly and controlled environment.
Job details include; Microscope work, using inspection tools, handling small parts, assembly under microscope, and data entry into SPC software. Ensure company safety and housekeeping standards are maintained during work activities.

If you have any questions please feel free to call April at 651-633-4080 and email at april.brausen@randstadusa.com

Working hours: 1st shift: 6am-2:30pm

Candidate requirements;
Must have a High School diploma or equivalent GED
Must have a minimum of 4 months medical assembly experience using microscopes for assembly.
Must be very detail oriented.
Must be able to work with a microscope for 8+ hours a day.
Must be able to work while standing for up to 8+ hours a day.
