Job Description

Posted April 12, 2013. Position closes Monday, April 29th, 5pm.

The West Broadway Business and Area Coalition (WBC) is seeking a qualified and motivated individual to fill our Aggregation and Distribution Coordinator position.

WBC is hiring a part-time coordinator to manage the aggregation table at the North Minneapolis farmers markets and then work to distribute that locally grown produce to other area businesses.
This is a part-time seasonal position May 2013-April 2014. The position will be about 20 hours per week. The pay is $15 per hour. Hours are flexible except every Tuesday (9:30am-1:30pm), Thursday (2:30pm-7:30pm) and Friday (1pm-8pm) during farmers market season.
Duties: (this is not an all-inclusive list) North Minneapolis Farmers Markets

  • Setting up a second year of the aggregation table at the West Broadway Farmers Market and/or mini markets
  • Attaining necessary project licensing and insurance while following all safety guidelines and procedures
  • Staffing the aggregation table
  • Fostering relationships with local and/or Northside growers
  • Distributing produce from the aggregation table or wholesalers to the corner stores on an as-needed basis
  • Researching best prices for the best growing produce and ordering wholesale produce when necessary
  • Promotion of the aggregation table as a resource to local vendors
  • Connecting members of the local foods resource Hub and Northside Fresh to the aggregation table and Community Table Cooperative

Distribution

  • Fostering existing relationships with corner store owners and employees
  • Train store owners and employees on produce handling and sales
  • Selling locally-grown produce and locally produced foods in corner stores, and other small accounts like restaurants
  • Encouraging and facilitating the donation to emergency food programs of local produce that is not sold to either small accounts or aggregators (ie. North Point Health and Wellness Inc.’s food shelf)

Research & Documentation

  • Work with other grant partners to ensure a comprehensive research and documentation process.
  • Assist partners with community organizing to create a core group of constituents and stakeholders (including organizations, residents, businesses, schools, etc.) to draft policy recommendations to the City of Minneapolis
  • Assist in convening a network of North Minneapolis growers/producers and vendors/sellers to collect process analysis information and successes/challenges of the relationships.
  • Collect pertinent project data and document project processes.
  • And other duties as assigned

Requirements:

  • Friendly and outgoing
  • Customer service skills and experience
  • Detail oriented with secure cash handling experience
  • Hold a valid drivers license and have access to a vehicle that can haul produce year-round
  • Ability to lift and move 50lbs
  • Ability to multi-task
  • An interest in healthy food access, farmers markets and locally grown food; experience as a customer or as a vendor at a farmers market.
  • An interest/passion in farmers markets, local economic development, and place-making
  • Flexible, organized, and able to prioritize

Preferred:

  • Ability to speak languages such as Hmong, Spanish, Somali, etc.
  • Northside resident or someone who has worked with the community
  • Interest in starting a food distribution business
  • Experience working across cultures and with diverse communities

Reports to:
